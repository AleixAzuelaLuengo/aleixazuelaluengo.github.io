Synopsis

Apart is a 2d puzzle-platformer game that grabs inspiration from games like Celeste, Guacamelee, and Braid.
You will find yourself in a minimalist world, where you will have to overcome a variety of difficult platforming challenges in order to advance.

Throughout your journey, you will unlock new abilities that will expand your mobility, using them wisely will be mandatory so you can conquer all the challenges you will encounter.

Game Design

One of the first things that I did during the development of Apart, was to do research and gather information about multiple titles across diferent generes. The idea was to, from this research, come up with the initial idea of the game we were gonna build for our dissertation.

After the initial research and pitch, we researched the market for further reference inside the 2D Puzzle-Platformer genere, comming with the initial version of our game.

Level Design

Kauil's Treasure's gameplay heavily relays on Puzzles. They're present in all the levels and they're crucial to be able to advance through the level. The puzzles were thought out cautiously to follow a correct progression and to be easy enough to fit our target.

Level Design

All the Levels on Kauil's Treasure follow a strict rule, they have to be intuitive so the players won't get lost. Having that in mind first we made a rough sketch on paper, to then make a greybox of the level on 3dsMax to see if the layout worked and then we implemented that greybox on Unity, to then test the level and iterate over it.

Once finished the iteration process we would implement the final version of the level. The levels I designed had less intuitive solutions, and sometimes that appeared to be a problem for the testers, but after adding a extra layer of hints and Player Guidance the players seemed to be less lost.
